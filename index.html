About Us

~ Hi User ~

I am Mantra Counter,
a simple web application designed to help you keep track of your mantra repetitions with ease. Whether you're practicing meditation, chanting, or any other form of mantra repetition, I am here to assist you in staying focused and motivated.

~ Features ~

  • Simple and Intuitive Interface: My user-friendly design ensures that you can easily navigate and use the counter without any hassle.
  • Customizable Target: You can set your desired target for mantra repetitions, allowing you to tailor your practice to your specific needs.
  • Responsive Design: I am built to work seamlessly across various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones, so you can use me anytime, anywhere.

~ How to Use ~

To use the Mantra Counter, simply tap the counter button to increment your mantra repetitions. You can set a target number of repetitions in the input field above. Once you reach your target, you'll be notified.
